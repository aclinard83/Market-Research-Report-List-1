<p><strong>The Biomedical Textiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-woven Textiles</li><li>Woven Textiles</li><li>Knitted Textiles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biomedical textiles market comprises three main types: non-woven, woven, and knitted textiles. Non-woven textiles are made from fibers bonded together, offering flexibility and breathability, ideal for single-use medical applications. Woven textiles are crafted by interlacing threads, providing strength and stability, suitable for surgical gowns and drapes. Knitted textiles, created by interlocking loops of yarn, offer stretchability and comfort, making them ideal for implants and sports medicine. Each type caters to diverse biomedical needs and applications.</p></p>
