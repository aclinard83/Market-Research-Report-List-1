<p><p>The automotive aluminum market is categorized into three main types: cast aluminum, rolled aluminum, and extruded aluminum. Cast aluminum is used for complex shapes and components, offering high strength and lightweight properties. Rolled aluminum, processed into sheets and foils, is primarily utilized for body panels and structural elements, providing durability and formability. Extruded aluminum involves shaping aluminum into specific profiles, ideal for applications like chassis and frame components, combining strength with reduced weight for improved fuel efficiency.</p></p>
