<p><ul><li>Silica Fiber/Glass Fiber Optics</li><li>Plastic Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical fiber optics market is primarily divided into two types: silica fiber (glass fiber) optics and plastic fiber optics. Silica fiber optics utilize glass for superior performance, offering high durability, low loss, and excellent imaging capabilities, making them ideal for minimally invasive procedures and diagnostic applications. Conversely, plastic fiber optics are more flexible, lightweight, and cost-effective, often used in applications where surgical precision and high performance are less critical. Both types play crucial roles in modern medical technology, enhancing imaging and communication.</p></p>
